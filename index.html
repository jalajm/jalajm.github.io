About Me

I am a graduate student in Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics at the University of Pennsylvania. I am available for full-time positions starting May 2017.

I am proficient in finite element analysis(FEA), computational fluid dynamics(CFD), numerical simulation and computational modeling. I am interested in working in areas dealing with design and development of automobile and automotive systems, numerical simulation, mathematical modeling, and control systems. My personal interests include music composition and playing (can play guitar, bass and piano), trekking, Formula 1 and football.

I am a motivated, ambitious, hard working, and an overall amiable individual. I am organized, capable of managing multiple projects at a time, have an eye for detail and a knack for looking at the big picture. Through my projects, I have highlighted my research skills and interests, as well as the ability to work individually and in a team. I am looking to put my diverse skills to use in a challenging opportunity in a research or product development role.
