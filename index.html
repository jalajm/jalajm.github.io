About Me

Hi, I am Jalaj! I have a master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics from the University of Pennsylvania. I’m actively seeking full-time opportunities in a product design, research, and product development role.

Through my academic coursework, research, and professional experience, I have acquired cutting edge skills essential in a challenging engineering work environment. My career interests are in autonomous systems, safe autonomy, robotics, perception, planning, control, and product development. I have explored areas with applications of machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.Being a technology enthusiast, I seek new and diverse skills and look to implement the same in real world applications continually.

I have extensive experience working as a Research Engineer in injury prevention, having worked on projects dealing with driving simulator data, designing systems for physical sled testing, and computational modeling.

I’m looking for an opportunity that is closely related to the above-mentioned areas of engineering. I am a motivated, ambitious, hardworking, and an overall amiable individual. Being organized, capable of managing multiple projects at a time, having an eye for detail and a knack for looking at the big picture are some other traits I possess. Through my projects, I have highlighted my research skills and interests, as well as the ability to work individually and in a team.

In my personal life, I enjoy reading about entrepreneurial ventures, watching movies, swimming, trekking, and experimenting with new and diverse cuisines. I thoroughly enjoy composing, recording, and listening to music. I have been playing the guitar, bass-guitar, and piano for over 7 years. Football and Formula-1 are my favorite sports.

Feel free to reach out to me. I love having a conversation!
